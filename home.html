About Me

Hey there! Glad you’re here! I’m someone who saw technology’s magic firsthand when I wrote my very first line of code and hit “run,” and in that moment everything clicked. Since then, I’ve been on a mission to turn that spark into a career, diving head-first into online courses and earning a spot in Adobe’s Digital Academy Software Engineer Immersive.

I’m driven by curiosity and love nothing more than breaking big problems into small, elegant solutions, and then building those solutions into apps people actually use. When I’m not at my keyboard, you’ll find me nodding along to everything from rock and pop to country and afrobeat (if it grooves, I’m listening), or cheering on my Detroit Lions back home in Michigan. Above all, I’m a reliable teammate who brings energy, empathy, and a genuine passion for making meaningful software. Let’s build something great together!
